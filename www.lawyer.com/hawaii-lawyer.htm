Hawaii Lawyer


In 1959, Hawaii became the 50th state. It has its own time zone and is the only state with an increasing landmass. The Big Island’s Lake Waiau is the world’s highest lake at 13,020 feet. The tallest coconut tree in Hilo is 92 feet, 5 inches. The Big Island exports the most macadamia nuts and orchids. HI produces over one-third of the world’s commercial pineapple supply. It is the only state that grows coffee.
