Massachusetts Lawyer


Massachusetts entered statehood in 1788. Its nicknames are “Puritan State” and “Baked Bean State.” The State Dog is the Boston terrier, and the State Dessert is Boston cream pie. Basketball was invented in Springfield in 1891, and volleyball was invented in Holyoke in 1895. Quincy is home to the first Dunkin Donuts and Howard Johnson Motel. Chocolate chip cookies were first made in Whitman.
