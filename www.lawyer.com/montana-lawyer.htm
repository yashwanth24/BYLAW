Montana Lawyer


Montana became a state in 1889. Its motto is “Oro y Plata,” or “Gold and Silver.” MT is home to Yellowstone, the country’s first national park. Montana has nearly 100 species of mammals, which is the most out of any state. The largest snowflake recorded (15 in.) fell in Montana. In 1888, Helena had more millionaires per capita than any other city. Dinosaur eggs were discovered at Egg Mountain in Choteau.
