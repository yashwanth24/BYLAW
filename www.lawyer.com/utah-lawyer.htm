Utah Lawyer


Utah’s nickname is the Beehive State. Its State Motto is “Industry.” The state’s citizens eat large quantities of Jello, making it the Jello Capital of the World. The mountains near Salt Lake City receive close to 500 inches of snowfall annually. Throughout Utah, annual precipitation ranges from under five inches in the deserts to over 60 inches in the mountains. The Great Salt Lake is saltier than the ocean.
