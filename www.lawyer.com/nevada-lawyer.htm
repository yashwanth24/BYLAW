Nevada Lawyer


Nevada’s name means “snow-covered” in Spanish. Nevada is the birthplace of tennis star Andre Agassi and former first lady Pat Nixon. Nevada produces more gold than any state in the nation. It is the world’s second largest gold producer behind South Africa. Las Vegas residents consume 60,000 pounds of shrimp daily – more than any other place in the country. Gambling was once illegal in Las Vegas.
