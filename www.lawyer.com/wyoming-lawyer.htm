Wyoming Lawyer


Wyoming is the 44th state. In 1872, Yellowstone became the first national park. Devils Tower became the first National Monument in 1906. WY was the first state to let women vote. In Wyoming, failure to close a fence carries a $750 fine. Kemmerer, the Fossil Fish Capital of the World, has over 100,000 fish fossils. JC Penney started in Wyoming. With 475,000 citizens, WY is the least populated state in the nation.
