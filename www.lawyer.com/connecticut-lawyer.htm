Connecticut Lawyer


Connecticut joined the nation in 1788. The first polaroid camera was invented in CT (1934), along with the first helicopter (1939) and first color television (1948). The first law school was established in Connecticut in 1784. In 1878, the New Haven District Telephone Company issued the first telephone book.
