New Hampshire Lawyer


New Hampshire was the 9th state. Famous citizens include Robert Frost and John Irving. The first public library was founded in Peterborough. The first potato was planted in Londonderry. NH was the first colony to declare independence from England. In 1934, the highest-ever ground wind speeds (231 MPH) were recorded at Mt. Washington. NH delegates were first to vote for the Declaration of Independence.
