introduction

Attorney David L. Freidberg is highly respected among prosecutors, judges and court personnel for his professionalism and relentless dedication to protecting the rights of his clients. From his offices in Chicago, Illinois, he serves clients in Cook County, DuPage County, Will County and Lake County, and is there for his clients 24 hours per day, seven days per week, via text, e-mail or telephone, and he provides the best customer service and response times in the business.

With more than 20 years of experience as a criminal defense attorney, he knows when to negotiate and when to fight against the charges. He fully understands court procedures, and because he doesn't pursue unreasonable or frivolous motions, prosecutors know they have a formidable opponent when David walks into the courtroom.
