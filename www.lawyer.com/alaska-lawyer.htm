Alaska Lawyer


Alaska is nicknamed “Land of the Midnight Sun.” It joined the US in 1959 as the 49th state. Alaska’s state fossil is the wooly mammoth, and its state sport is dog mushing. The largest cabbage on record -weighing 94 pounds- came from Alaska. There are 3 million lakes, 29 volcanoes, and over 100,000 glaciers in Alaska. During the 1897 Gold Rush, miners traded gold for nutritious potatoes.
