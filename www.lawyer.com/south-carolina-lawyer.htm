South Carolina Lawyer


South Carolina became a state in 1788. Its nicknames are “Palmetto State” and “Rice State.” The State Reptile is the loggerhead sea turtle, and the State Game Bird is wild turkey. Johnston is the Peach Capital of the World, and Wasmalaw Island has the only commercial tea plantation in America. Sumter has the world’s largest Gingko farm. The Edisto Memorial Gardens in Orangeburg have award-winning roses.
